Game Instructions

  • Click on the boxes to reveal what's on the map
  • If you click the bomb, you lose
  • If you click on a tree, go again
  • If you click on the treasure, you win

Game Rules

  1. You get 3 Chances to find the Treasure.
  2. There is no time limit.
